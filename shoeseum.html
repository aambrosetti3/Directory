Shoeseum

The ability to search and shop for products via mobile apps provides convenience and efficiency for the modern shopper. Having access to an entire inventory of shoe styles at one's fingertips provides a shopping experience with endless possibilities.

Limited inventory of shoe styles and sizes in brick and mortar stores also can lead to fruitless shopping trips. Having the ability to save and return to favorite styles, and being able to purchase and return items from home or anywhere on the go are some of the many benefits of a mobile shoe shop experience.

Objectives

  • Design a mobile app experience and interface for an online store.
  • Conceptualize a brand and establish guidelines to be reflected in the mobile app.

Role

UX/UI Designer, Brand designer

Tools & Timeline

Tools

  • Adobe XD
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Prott

    • Timeline

    2 weeks

    Process

    Research - Ideation - Wireframe - Design - Prototype

    Research

    Performing competitive/comparative analysis on other similar existing shoe shopping apps, I sought to identify features, user flows, and design aspects that were common, as well as those that I felt would or would not work with the app I was looking to design.

    Ideation

    Creating a brand vision and style came next, by defining the brand values and identity, as well as defining the user stories and user flow of the app I designed a logo and selected elements such as the color, typography, and imagery that created a sense of lightness, movement, and modernity.

    Wireframe

    Taking the user flow I began sketching low-fidelity wireframes, setting up the overall screen layouts and blocking areas for elements and components. Narrowing in on the overall design, I converted these to more mid-fidelity wireframes in Adobe XD, and set up basic prototypes with these wireframes for users to test.

    Design

    Analyzing the user feedback from testing the mid-fidelity prototypes, I incorporated specific changes that helped improve the experience. I then continued to fill in the branding for all elements, styling it to fit the brand vision I had earlier defined.

    Prototype

    With the high-fidelity wireframes completed, I finalized the prototyping between the detailed screens and created presentable mockups within device frames to showcase the final look of the app.
